My Favourite Romantic Movie

Me before you is one of my favourite movies of all time. The plot is based on a romantic novel from 2012 by Jojo Moyes.
The GOT star Emilia Clark is playing the main role where she is a spirited 26-year-old from a struggling middle-class family.
While most love stories have a happy ending, this story depicts how even true love has its limits and sad endings.
